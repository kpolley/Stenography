Stenography

Stenography is the practice concealing a message within another message or object. Simply put, it is hiding information in plain sight such that only the intended recipient would get to see it.

One common (and fun) form of stenography is to embed a message into an image. This works by replacing a handful of image bits with that of the message. It barely alters the image itself and the modification cannot be seen by the human eye.
